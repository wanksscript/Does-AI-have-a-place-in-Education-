Introduction

ChatGPT is an AI chatbot that allows users to perform tasks, research, and compose text such as essays and write code. But this resource can be taken advantage of by students who don’t know how to use it properly.

This PAT will assess the effects of ChatGPT in school environment and how it affects both teachers and students alike.
